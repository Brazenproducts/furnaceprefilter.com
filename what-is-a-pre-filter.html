What Is a Pre-Filter?

What an HVAC pre-filter is, how it works, and why pairing a MERV 8 pre-filter with a MERV 13 main filter saves you money.

A pre-filter protects the expensive stage

An HVAC pre-filter catches the coarse junk first so the main filter does not load up as quickly. In homes running premium MERV 13 media, that matters a lot. Letting a cheaper MERV 8 stage catch lint, visible dust, and pet hair first is a straightforward way to improve economics and reduce waste.
